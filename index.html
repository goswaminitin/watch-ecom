Our Heritage

Crafted with Purpose

Since 1892, Chronos has been at the forefront of horological innovation. What began in a small workshop in the Swiss Jura mountains has evolved into one of the most respected names in luxury watchmaking.

Every timepiece bearing the Chronos name undergoes over 400 hours of meticulous hand-assembly and testing, ensuring that each watch meets our uncompromising standards of quality and precision.

Precision

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Every Chronos timepiece comes with a comprehensive 5-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects and movement issues. Our warranty is honored at any of our authorized service centers worldwide. Extended warranty options up to 10 years are available at the time of purchase.

We recommend servicing your mechanical watch every 4-5 years. You can bring it to any authorized Chronos boutique or service center, or request our insured collection service. Service includes complete movement overhaul, case and bracelet refinishing, and water-resistance testing.

Yes, we offer flexible financing through our partnership with leading financial institutions. Interest-free installments are available for up to 24 months on select timepieces. Apply during checkout for instant approval with no impact on your credit score.

We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. The watch must be in its original, unworn condition with all accompanying documentation and packaging. Returns are processed within 5-7 business days, and full refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Select models offer personalization options including caseback engraving, custom dial configurations, and bespoke strap selections. Our Atelier service provides fully bespoke commissions for clients seeking a truly unique timepiece. Please contact our concierge team for details.

Water resistance varies by model. Our dress watches are rated to 30m (splash-proof), sport models to 100m, and dive watches to 300m or deeper. Each watch undergoes ISO 22810 testing. We recommend annual water-resistance checks if you regularly expose your watch to water.
